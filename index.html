History of Nellai
Karupatti coffee

India is a country known for its Heritage and culture. In the initial days of our lives, our grandma brewed us coffee with freshly made palm jaggery. The ‘Original’ karupatti coffee. Every sip had a unique flavour and was full of richness. Great memories, Great taste. In today’s world, full of trends and growing popularity of western food culture, we want to remain classic and decided to revive the sweet memories. This led to the idea ofNELLAI KARUPATTI COFFEE.

About Vamil Groups (NKC)

Vamil Groups(NELLAI KARUPATTI COFFEE)is an authentic coffee outlet. We are known for our signature Karupatti coffee and other Karupatti infused snacks, which are both traditional and tasty.

To ‘Serve Healthy’ is our priority and ‘Your Satisfaction’ is our aim.

Our vision is to serve healthy organic food and reintroduce the traditional food system. With the dawn of the modern era, we are committed to bring back authentic foods infused with innovation.

Our Diverse menu

At Vamil Groups, a branch of Nellai Karupatti Coffee, we've blended tradition with modernity to create something truly special. O ur journey began with a vision to explorethe rich potential of palm jaggery, leading us to craft an array of sweets and savories. What started small has now blossomed into over 80 delectable treats, all made exclusively from palm and sugarcane jaggery. Join us for a delightful experience that merges heritage with innovation. Indulge in our diverse menu featuring millet food, palm jaggery coffee, tea, ice creams, and more
